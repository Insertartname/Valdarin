13.8 Billion

Lovers Bahamut and Tiamat are born from the nothingness; they have a desire for love and creation.

12.1 Billion

Planes are created

11 Billion

The first Deities: Felorai Painter (Jocasta in common), Hardri Weaver (Mina), Siksta Jimosar (Sol), and Welun Weaver (Solana) are born.

8 Billion

Betrayer Deities: Exalted, Loerchik, and Elise are created from the void.

4.5 Billion

Planets are created, and along with them, Wer Irral (Val'darin, meaning true love).

4.4 Billion

The Skjall Di Tobor (Tree of Life) is planted

3.9 Billion

Kethend (Gem) Dragons are created to roam the realms

3.5 Billion

Primitive life is born

3.3 Billion

The Chaos War, a battle between Chaos and Life, begins when Exalted attacks Mina.

1.1 Billion

The Chaos War ends, and the dragons imprison each Betrayer Deity in different planes and multiverses.

1 Billion

Infected by the Void, Tiamat attacks Bahamut

700 Million

Bahamut, with no other choice, imprisons Tiamat in the lowest plane.

690 Million

Tiamat manipulates this plane, creating the circles of Hell, creating demons, and birthing Chromatic Dragons. These colorful dragons and fiends represent the flaws of Bahamut.

680 Million

In response, Bahamut creates angels and metallic dragons. These polished dragons and celestials represent the positive qualities of Tiamat.

679 Million

The Infinite battle between celestials and fiends begins.

100 Million - 10 Million BCE

Giants terrorize ancient species, using them like working animals or cattle. Gem dragons wage war on giants, protecting humanoids and watching over them as they grow.

Roughly 5 - 2 Million BCE

The first oldest species begins to evolve: Dwarf, Elf, Orc, and Dragonborn. It is largely debated how many cultures have their theories, but all agree that it started in Fedinia.

2 Million - 900,000 BCE

Other species such as the Humans, Gnomes, Triton, and Animal-Folk evovle. It is debated once again how many cultures have their theories, but all agree that it started in Fedinia. It is confirmed around this time Tritons depart from the land and remain in the sea.

500,000 - 200,000 BCE

First settlements are formed, and dragons teach humanoids draconic.

180,000 - 60,000 BCE

Species begin to migrate out from Fedinia into ancient Astra, Odron, Shannar, and Heavena

12,000 - 7,000 BCE

Development of agriculture and metalworking began.

6,481 BCE

Bronze Age begins, Foligrove (translating to Forest of Birth) is founded, and civilizations develop.

4000 BCE

The first sorcerer, Charis (Hope), is born with immense power after absorbing a dragon's soul.

3000 BCE

The first documentation of species learning how to manipulate the weave.

2000 - 1000 BCE

First druidic circle of the Sheperd, drudic language is documented, and the first Archdruid named Willow Seeker.

800 - 400 BCE

The first cleric domains (Life and Nature), Ranger Conclaves (Hunter and Beastermaster) are formed.

350 - 50 BCE

Orcs and goblins assault ancient astrans, learning to channel the weave and anger, creating the first Primal Path of Berserker.

5000 - 3000 AD

Ancient Astrans, humans, and elves agree to form a treaty to fight back against the orcs. Additionally, the first magical school is established, it would later become Lewis Institute of Magical Arts.

1521 AD

A group of travelers finds themselves on an island, which turns out to be a dragon turtle. It teaches them the ways of ki, and they become the first monks.

1400 AD

The first Ancient Oath for Paladins is written, and the Domain of Order is the official domain of Heavena.

1250 AD

Heavenians begin to contact celestials, and the Acension platform is created on the tallest mountain. Meeting angels for the first time and blessing them, some of the purest individuals are turned into Nephilim.

1000 AD

Some Heavenian citizens are morphed into Tielfings, there is no agreement on how this happen.

950 AD

The Silver Order is formed and, with permission from the church, starts the Tielfing Trials, driving many of them to flee.

800 AD

A powerful Sorcerer known as G.Victor finds the Necronomicon. Performing the ritual, he becomes the first vampire and plagues the land. This sparks the Age of Explorers

700 AD

The economy of dungeon explorers and guilds booms, the first monster manual is created, and blood gems are found. These gems contain the blood of G.Victor infused with powerful dark magic. Planar Travel is created through Woven Gates using these gems but Victor uses this to create demonic portals.

699 AD

The Mage Council is established.

680 AD

G.Victor is defeated by adventurers and imprisoned in the Shadowfell planes. The Necronomicon is taken by the Mage Coucnil and sealed away in their main headquarters.

500 AD

A warlock and a powerful demon attempt to merge hell and the material realm.

495 AD

They are defeated, the demon fragments his power and spreads it throughout the world.

300 AD

Struilia is created as a sanctuary state for tielfings by Odron and Fedinia. A young woman names Venessa achieves lichdom in Heavena, this sparks the Rune Wars. To defeat Venessa, adventurers must collect ancient weapons.

299 AD

The Mage council develops classifications of magic and bans Necromancy.

295 AD

Venessa ascends to a demi-god through the assistance of a woman vampire and worshippers.

293 AD

Astra takes over Shannar, bans all mages and magic from their nation.

250 AD

The Astran Guantlet has started to train future astrans to be better than everyone else. No weakness allowed.

1500s

Technology booms, Odron takes the charge by revolutionizing civilization through technology and creating Artificers.

1600s

The first warforged are built to help with daily tasks and jobs. Thieves Guild rises in numbers due to some citizens losing their jobs.

1700s

A vampire named Victoria Fang starts a calamity by sending an army of undead into nations. Warforged are used for combat and to defeat her alongside 6 adventurers.

1800s

The Calamity Wars begin in 1819 to 1830, and guilds begin to fight over fame and territory. A wizard named Ed Nom, guild leader of the Circular Fire, creates the rules for Guild Wars and establishes the Arcanic Steel Tournament. The guild Destiny Knights win.

1900s

Feywild individuals begin to immigrate, bringing Satyrs, Centaurs, Eldarin, etc. They start colleges for Bards.

2000

The second Arcanic Steel is declared, overseen by Ed Nom once again. Phoenix Wings wins.

2010s

Orc nation of Kaiva launches an attack on Odron, sparking the Great Waugh War.

2025

Kaiva surrenders.

2060s

An orc calling himself Zagnith the Conquerer ascends, kills the previous deity of war and starts another Great Waugh War.

2065

Odron drops the first mag-bomb, a nuclear weapon, on Kaiva. This didn't stop the orcs.

2066

Odron drops another bomb, and the Orcs surrender. Citizens of Kaiva immigrate to other areas, largely being absorbed by Astra. The United Council is established to uphold all nations and prevent tragedies.

2068

Struilia is given independence and becomes an official nation. The Tiefling Trials officially end.

2090

Warforged are given independence from Odron and are recognized as Humanoids.

2199

An 8-year-old adventurer going by Jalen "Red" Evenbright is chosen as the new leader of the Phoenix Wings.

2200

Ed Nom starts the Arcanic Tournament III, Red exposes that Ed Nom is an Illrigger. Ed Nom is a Demon, Red wins the tournament, and defeats Ed Nom. The Mage Council captures Ed Nom and brings him to their headquarters to study. Mage Council revises the rules of Guild Wars and puts them on a Demi-plane

2220

Red disbands Phoenix Wing and creates Color Theory with: Rachel (Blue) Chelsa, Rex (Green) Chelsa, David (Orange) Lightjumper, Kevin (White)Gannon, Lilith (purple) Lunarflow, and Makaveli (Yellow) Evermint.

2235

Red goes on a mission and never returns. Color Theory disbands.

2297

Astra begins to expand into Fedinia

2300

Death Blossom campaign starts here. Please take a look at the recap for the extended history of events. On May 28th, the Blight attacks a Struilian city and levels it. The Blight is defeated thanks to the Wild Hunt, apart of the Freelancers.

2301

The Price of a Soul and The Hunt take place

2302

Artificial Flavors takes place. The company Igris sues Hemogoblin for having blood in Blood X, and additionally accuses their CEO of being a vampire.

2303

A Worthy Challenge takes place.

2304

Rebellion takes place, Igancio becomes the new king of Astra. Eight months later, Cozy Days take place.

2305

New campaign starts here.
